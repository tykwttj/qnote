## Full Description

Qnote is the fastest, most beautiful way to capture your thoughts. Organize everything with color-coded categories and find any note in seconds.

**WHY QNOTE?**

- Lightning fast - opens instantly, auto-saves as you type
- 9 beautiful colors to categorize your notes at a glance
- Modern, clean design that's a joy to use
- Home screen widgets to access notes without opening the app

**FEATURES**

- Create unlimited text notes
- Color-code with 9 vibrant categories
- Pin important notes to the top
- Real-time search across all notes
- Swipe to delete or pin
- Dark mode (auto or manual)
- List or grid view
- Share notes to any app
- Trash with 30-day recovery
- 3 sizes of home screen widgets
- Sort by date, title, or color
- Adjustable font size
- Works offline - your data stays on your device

**ORGANIZE YOUR LIFE**

Whether you're jotting down a shopping list, saving meeting notes, or capturing a brilliant idea - Qnote helps you find it later with color tags and instant search.

**PRIVACY FIRST**

All notes are stored locally on your device. No account required. No cloud sync means no data leaks.

Download Qnote now and start organizing your thoughts beautifully.
